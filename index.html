Don Hoopengarner - My Story

I am naturally a handy-man. Through over 45 years of home ownership, I have changed plumbing fixtures, cut in windows and window trim, added tile floors, installed a fireplace mantle, installed doors, changed interior and exterior light fixtures, painted interior and exterior walls, fixed trims, re-caulked bathrooms, and repaired faucets. I have changed garbage disposals, installed electrical outlets, built garage shelving and workbenches and decks. I have repaired oven controls, refrigerator controls, fixed fences and installed thermostats. I can perform minor automotive and RV repairs. I take on repair projects because they are there, and my hobby has always been to fix things.


I can’t stand something not working and have never called in professional help, following in my fathers footsteps. I have lots of old tools (and some newer ones too), because I like to fix things myself. I get a real sense of accomplishment fixing things and helping others. As my 50 year career in the commercial temperature controls business comes to its conclusion, with all of my construction and retrofit experience in building, trains, cruiseships, I would really enjoy the feeling of building community and social interaction by helping people when things don’t work, and they are in need of help from someone who naturally enjoys fix-it projects.
